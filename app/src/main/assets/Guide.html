Guide

Login Portal

If you don't register already, just click on 'REGISTER' button and follow the steps. If you register already then login with registered mail ID and password.

Registration Portal

Fill the all necessary fields with valid data. Don't fill empty values and random values to it.

Phone Authentication Portal

If you are login/register with your mobile number, it allow only one query process for that user. You just verify your mobile number that's it. But, we recommend login/register with valid mail ID.

Verifying the user

Once you registered to the app, it redirect to the home page of the application. Their is 'Verify' button. Click on it, then open 'Gmail' then choose account which you registered with app. Then app name email appear, open it and click to verify.

Re-login user

When you verify the mail go to app, tap on three horizontal line on left hand at top. In this section basic functionility of application is located. Tap on 'Sign Out' and again login in with verified mail and registered password.

Accessing the function's

When you verify mail and again login in application, now you are accessible the all functions of application.
